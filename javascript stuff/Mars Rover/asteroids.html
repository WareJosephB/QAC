Raise the temperature of Mars by firing your Laser Blaster

Make oceans of blood by killing foes

Collect plants to oxygenate the atmosphere

Steel powerups give you armour, titanium powerups make you faster

Press F/T to use Steel/Titanium (you can have up to 2 titanium and 1 steel active at a time)

Don't fall off the edge of Mars before your mission is done.

Good luck
